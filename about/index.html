About

Photograph by Zeeshan Younus

Hello, I'm Waqas Ahmed. A multi-disciplinary designer based in Dubai, UAE working with clients remotely and locally. Over the years I’ve worked in a variety of roles encompassing design, development and all things digital.

In this time, I’ve been blessed to work for some of the world’s most admired people. My process starts with a pencil and a blank paper which leads to low and high fidelity wire-framing, information architecture, and user experience research whether it's a small Mobile App Design or a large project.

When I’m not sitting at my computer, I’m often posting images to my Instagram or Tweeting.
